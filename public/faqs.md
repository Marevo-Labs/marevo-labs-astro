## Working with us

### Do I have to be technically savvy to work with you?
Not at all! We are here to help you every step of the way, and will translate technical jargon into plain English that anyone can understand. We will guide you through the process of building your website, from the initial design to the final launch. We will also help you with the technical aspects of your website, such as setting up your domain name, hosting your website, and managing your website content.

### Do you only do phone/video calls, or can we meet in person?
We are happy to meet in person, or we can do phone/video calls. We are based in Manchester and Northville, Michigan, and regularly work with businesses in Ann Arbor, Plymouth, Canton, Novi, Saline, Dexter, and Chelsea. We are also happy to work with you remotely wherever you are in the world, and we can provide you with a secure and private communication channel to keep your project confidential.

### Do I need my own domain name and hosting to work with you?
No, we can help you with both, as well as any other technical aspects of your website or app. We can be hands off and just hand over code, or we can offer white-glove service and handle everything for you.

### Do you offer SEO services?
Of course! We offer a variety of SEO services to help you get more traffic to your website. We will help you optimize your website for search engines, and we will also help you with your content strategy to help you get more traffic to your website.

### Can you do my social media marketing for me?
Currently, we do not offer social media marketing services, but we can help you with your content strategy to help you get more traffic to your website. We can also help connect you with partners who offer social media marketing services.

### I have an idea for a website or app, but I don't know how to get started. Can you help me?
Yes, we can help you with market research and product viability analysis. If you have a rough idea of what you want, we can help you refine it and make it more viable, and if it makes sense, we can help you get started.

## Why Our Pricing Plans Offer Great Value

We believe in providing exceptional value at every price point. Our pricing plans are designed to cater to a variety of needs, from individuals and small teams to large enterprises. Each plan includes access to our powerful features, seamless integrations, and top-notch customer support.

## Understanding Our Pricing Plans

### What is difference between the Lump Sum and Monthly plan?
The biggest difference is the short-term versus long-term costs. With the lump sum, you pay a one-time fee for the project, while with the monthly plan, you are spreading out the cost over time. Over time you'll likely save money with the lump sum, but paying a smaller monthly fee is more manageable for many businesses. It all comes down to cash flow and short- versus long-term goals.

### Can I add on 24/7 Support, Lifetime Updates, or other features to the Lump Sum plan?
Yes, we offer add-ons to any of our plans, typically with a 12 month commitment. This helps us plan for the future and ensures we can continue to support you long-term. We can also work out a custom plan for you if you need something specific.

### Can I change or cancel my Monthly subscription plan at any time?
The Monthly plan is contract-based and typically starts with a minimum 12 month commitment, and after that is month to month. You can cancel anytime after the contract commitment time has passed. If you do decide to cancel, we have options to help you with the transition, whether that is shutting down the site, paying a one-time transfer fee, or other options.

### What happens if I cancel my Monthly subscription plan before the contract commitment time has passed?
We prioritize building long-term relationships with our clients, and hope our clients reciprocate. We also understand that sometimes things change out of our control. Typically, you would be responsible for the full remaining contract amount, but we are happy to find a solution that works for both parties.

### How do additional pages work?
Many simple websites require 5 or less pages, but we find that a lot of websites need more than 5 pages, and we offer a $100/page fee after the first 5 pages. This is a one-time fee, and you can add as many pages as you need. There is no limit to the number of pages you can add, and we can offer discounts for larger projects.

### What payment methods do you accept?
Our invoicing software Wave securely handles the payments, and you can pay with Apple Pay or any major card: Visa, Visa Debit, Mastercard, Mastercard Debit, and American Express. Invoices are sent via email, and you can also set up recurring payments for the Monthly plan.

### Can you tell me more about the Custom plan?
Some projects are just more complex than others, whether that is due to complex business logic, custom API integrations, building out an e-commerce store, scaffolding a Content Management System (CMS), or other unique requirements. With nearly two decades of experience each, we have the expertise to handle even the most complex projects. We offer a custom plan for these projects, and we can work with you to design a plan that works best for you.
